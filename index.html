Instructions for Adding Your Signature

Windows - Outlook: After copying the signature, open Outlook, go to "File" > "Options" > "Mail" > "Signatures." Create a new signature and paste the copied signature into the editor. You may need to use the "Paste Special" option and select "Merge Formatting" to maintain the green color on the first line.

Mac - Outlook & Mail App: After copying the signature, open the signature settings in either Outlook or Mail, create a new signature, and paste the copied signature.

iOS - Mail App: On your iOS device, copy the signature and go to "Settings" > "Mail" > "Signature." Paste the signature into the signature field.
