Instructions for Adding Your Signature

Windows: Outlook

  • Go to File > Options > Mail.
  • Click on "Signatures..." to open the Signatures and Stationery window.
  • Create a new signature or edit an existing one, and paste the copied signature into the editor.
  • If the green color on the first line is lost, use the "Paste Special" function and choose "Merge Formatting" to retain the color.
  • Click OK to save your changes.

Mac: Outlook

  • Go to Outlook > Preferences > Signatures.
  • Create a new signature or select an existing one to edit.
  • Paste the copied signature into the editor.
  • Close the Signatures window to save your changes.

Mac: Mail App

  • Go to Mail > Preferences > Signatures.
  • Select the email account you want to add the signature to.
  • Create a new signature or select an existing one to edit.
  • Paste the copied signature into the editor.
  • Close the Preferences window to save your changes.

iOS: Mail App

  • Go to Settings > Mail > Signature.
  • Choose whether to apply the signature to all accounts or just specific ones.
  • Tap and hold in the signature field, then paste the copied signature.
  • Close the Settings app to save your changes.
